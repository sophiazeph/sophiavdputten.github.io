About

I'm a nonbinary maker, performer and teacher. My work moves at the intersection of theatre, dance and visual art, with a fascination for bodily transformations that unsettle and captivate. I believe that it is precisely in that in-between space, where repulsion and attraction meet, that transformation becomes possible. When we don't try to resolve the tension between opposites right away, but instead allow it to exist, a dynamic emerges in which perception can shift.

In 2023 I surprised audiences with Fluidical at Amsterdam Fringe Festival, a solo performance with a dancer and 150 litres of slime, and in 2024 flowers grew from my body in the surrealist performance Botaniek (Botanical).

Stichting Landing & Zeelanding

Together with artistic partner and sister Julia van der Putten, I founded organisation Stichting Landing with which we create and facilitate innovative performing arts. One of our projects is Zeelanding: a platform for artistic experiment in community in our home region of Zeeuws-Vlaanderen (province of Zeeland), through which we organise the biennial Zeelanding Festival.
