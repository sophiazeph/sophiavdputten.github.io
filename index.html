About

I'm a nonbinary maker, performer and teacher. My work moves at the intersection of theatre, dance and visual art, with a fascination for bodily transformations that unsettle and captivate.

I believe that it is precisely in that in-between space, where repulsion and attraction meet, that transformation becomes possible. When we don't try to resolve the tension between opposites right away, but instead allow it to exist, a dynamic emerges in which perception can shift.Read more »
